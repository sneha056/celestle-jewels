Exchange and return policy

We have a 7 day money back policy which allows you to ensure your purchase is secure and satisfactory. When the purchased item is returned, the product is inspected by our quality control department for any signs of tampering. Only items returned in perfect condition with all tags and protective materials intact are eligible for a refund. Refunds can be credited to your account at our store for future purchase or can be refunded to your bank account, depending on the payment method.


Lifetime exchange & buy-back policy

We provide a lifetime exchange & buy-back policy for all the purchases within India. At the time of buy back, items can either be exchanged or encashed. They will be valued at the current market rates. The credited value for exchanged items, after deduction, will be added to your account with us for future purchases$. If you choose to get your jewellery encashed, the amount will be transferred to your bank account within 10 days of receiving the product. Returned jewellery undergoes quality control before credits or refunds are processed.
